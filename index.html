Brilliance, Passion & Purpose

The NYCER Foundation is a non-profit body composed of a group of young researchers from the various computer science and engineering domain to promote collaborative interdisciplinary research and development.

OUR VISION

The NYCER Foundation exists to empower young minds in computer science by building sustainable collaborative networks that amplify individual impact and drive innovation in socially, economically and environmentally responsible research areas.

OUR MISSION

The NYCER Foundation aims to

BUILD A SELF-SUSTAINING NETWORK OF BRILLIANT MINDS IN COMPUTING
AMPLIFY THE IMPACT OF YOUNG INDIVIDUAL INNOVATORS IN TECHNOLOGY
ADDRESS PRESSING SOCIAL, ECONOMIC & ENVIRONMENTAL CHALLENGES THROUGH TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION
UPHOLD ETHICAL STANDARDS BY PROMOTING MORALLY GROUNDED ADVANCES IN COMPUTING

OUR FOUNDERS

Dr. Ayan Kumar Panja
Treasurer

Dr. Swarnendu Ghosh
President
Dr. Swarnendu Ghosh specializes in computer vision, deep learning, and generative AI. His scholarly contributions range from neural architectures and image segmentation to graph-based representation learning.
Dr. Deepsubhra Guha Roy
